Brain Tumor Detection

Brain cancer is one of the dangerous diseases globally. Various image techniques
such as Computed Tomography (CT), Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI)
and ultrasound image are used to evaluate the tumor in a brainIn this work,
automatic brain tumor detection is proposed by using Convolutional Neural
Networks (CNN) classification. It helps in early detection of brain tumors
